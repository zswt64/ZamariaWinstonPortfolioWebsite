Who Is Zamaria?





All of the above! Zamaria Winston is a second-year Computational Media major at the Georgia Institute of Technology. With threads in Media and Interaction Design, she plans to better the user experience of those in under represented communities. Applications do not always account for women or people of color while being tested and processed. Therefore, entire demographics of people are not being considered. In her career,she will make steps towards bettering this gap between the user and the interface designer.


Prior to her journey at Georgia Tech, Zamaria indulged in Audio-Video Technology and Film at her alma mater, Westlake High School. While directing and editing weekly video segments for the school's youtube account, Zamaria catered to the students' desires for entertaining content which contributed to her now interest in user experience. Aside from her fulfilling experience in AV Tech, she also earned the title of Salutatorian in her graduating class of 2021.


While Zamaria is proud of her previous accomplishments in high school, she aims to find a higher success at Georgia Tech. In utilizing the abundance of resources around her, Zamaria will reach her Tech goals and, most importantly, make a difference in her surrounding communities.
